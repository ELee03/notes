Field Notes
A personal collection of annotated notes on subjects worth understanding — written to build intuition, not to prepare for tests.
Topics span engineering, mathematics, and the sciences with no allegiance to any particular curriculum. Active clusters have published lessons; planned ones are indexed for future content.
Where relevant, connections to courses, textbooks, and other resources are noted inside each cluster — not as prerequisites, but as references.
