Embedded Systems
MCU Architecture · Peripherals · Protocols · Firmware · PCB Design
How hardware and software meet. These notes cover the full stack from silicon peripherals and communication protocols up through firmware architecture and PCB layout — with emphasis on understanding what's actually happening at the register and signal level, not just how to use a HAL.
The primary hardware platform is the Silicon Labs EFR32 Cortex-M33 series. All peripheral examples reference the EFR32 TRM and HAL, but the concepts apply to any ARM Cortex-M MCU. PCB design chapters are platform-independent and relevant to any mixed-signal or RF board.
